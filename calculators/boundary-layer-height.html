Boundary Layer Height

Estimate boundary-layer thickness using laminar Blasius, turbulent 1/7th-power-law, or turbulent 1/5th-power-law approximations.

Formulas

Blasius laminar

\[ \delta = \frac{ 5x }{ \sqrt{Re_x} } \]

Turbulent 1/7th law

\[ \delta = 0.16xRe_x^{-1/7} \]

Turbulent 1/5th law

\[ \delta = 0.37xRe_x^{-1/5} \]
