CFL Number Calculator

Calculate the Courant–Friedrichs–Lewy (CFL) number used in CFD stability analysis and time step selection.

The time step is generally selected such that the CFL number remains below 1 in the LES region of the computational domain. In practice, it is often recommended to target a CFL number of approximately 0.5 in order to account for differences between instantaneous and time-averaged velocities
