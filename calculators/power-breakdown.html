Power Breakdown Calculator

Estimate the percentage contribution of aerodynamic drag, wheel rotational losses, rolling resistance, wheel bearing losses, and climbing power across cycling speed. The power model was introduced by Martin et al. (1998).

Formulas

\[ P_{AD} = \frac{1}{2}\rho C_dA U^3 \] \[ P_{WR} = \frac{1}{2}\rho F_w U^3 \] \[ P_{RR} = U C_{rr}mg \] \[ P_{WB} = U(91 + 8.7U)\times 10^{-3} \] \[ P_{GR} = mgGU \] \[ P_{tot} = P_{AD} + P_{WR} + P_{RR} + P_{WB} + P_{GR} \] \[ \text{Power share} = 100\frac{P_i}{P_{tot}} \]
