Hydraulic Diameter Calculator

Calculate the hydraulic diameter of a non-circular duct or flow passage. Hydraulic diameter is commonly used in Reynolds number, pressure loss, and internal flow calculations when the cross-section is not circular.

← Back to Engineering Tools

Formula

\[ D_h = \frac{4A}{P} \]

where \(A\) is the cross-sectional flow area and \(P\) is the wetted perimeter.
