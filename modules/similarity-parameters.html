Similarity Parameters

Non-dimensional parameters used to characterize flow physics and determine dynamic similarity between experiments, simulations, and real-world flows. These quantities govern viscous, compressible, gravitational, and thermal scaling effects.

← Back to modules

Reynolds Number

Ratio of inertial to viscous forces.

Mach Number

Ratio of flow speed to speed of sound.

Strouhal Number

Non-dimensional frequency parameter for oscillating and vortex-shedding flows.

Froude Number

Ratio of inertial to gravitational effects.

Prandtl Number

Ratio of momentum diffusivity to thermal diffusivity.
